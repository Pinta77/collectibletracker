Rumble Robots Complete Setlist

Diamond Series was the second series of Rumble Robots trading cards to be released. Diamond Series scrapped the trading card game and was only used the Rumble Robots toys. Aside from the TCG elements being absent, Diamond Series is nearly identical to Base Series. Some cards now have different artwork featuring the Robots in their corresponding armor.

Barcodes for scanning are now found on the front and back of each card.
