EX 傳說的締造者 Set List

EX Legend Maker (EX 傳說的締造者) was released in 2006. It is the second Pokémon TCG set printed in Traditional Chinese. While there were only two sets localized in Traditional Chinese prior to 2019, some English TCG products were still released in Taiwan and Hong Kong.
