Rumble Robots Complete Setlist

Base Series was the first series of Rumble Robots trading cards to be released. This original series was used with Rumble Robots toys by scanning them, and was also used to play the trading card game. Later on, this series and the trading card game were scrapped and replaced with the Diamond Series, which was only for use with the Robot toys.

In the Diamond and Invasion series, the barcode for scans is on the front and back. However, for the Base Series, the barcode is only on the back of the card.
