Naknak Complete Setlist

Naknak by Hasbro was a collectible toy line released in 2001. Most of the figures could be obtained in booster packs sold in retail stores, however some figures such as the Creepnaks were exclusive to McDonald's happy meals. There are 93 unique figures, that can be seen below. Read more about Naknak here!
